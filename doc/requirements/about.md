# About Otaku Community Project

## Overview

**Otaku Community Project** is a community-driven platform designed for anime and manga enthusiasts to share content, discuss their favorite works, and connect with others who share similar interests. The project focuses on combining social features with structured anime/manga data to create richer, more meaningful user-generated content.

Our goal is to build a scalable, modern community platform that balances **discussion** , **content discovery** , and **structured knowledge** , while keeping the user experience simple and enjoyable.

---

## Vision

To become a trusted and engaging hub where otaku culture thrives through high-quality discussions, accurate anime/manga information, and strong community interactions.

---

## Core Principles

- **Community First** – Features are designed to encourage healthy discussion and interaction.
- **Content with Context** – Posts are enhanced by linking to anime and manga data.
- **Scalable by Design** – Architecture and APIs are built with long-term growth in mind.
- **Clean & Maintainable** – Emphasis on clear coding conventions and system design.

---

## Key Features

### 📝 Content & Posts

- Create and share posts related to anime, manga, and otaku culture
- Automatically or manually link posts to anime/manga data
- Rich post detail view with related references

### 🔗 Anime & Manga Integration

- Integration with external anime/manga data sources
- Structured references for anime and manga entities
- Enhanced discovery through linked content

### 💬 Chat & Interaction (MVP)

- One-to-one private chat
- Cursor-based pagination for messages
- Soft delete for messages

### 📊 Activity & System Logging

- User activity tracking for key actions
- System-level logging for monitoring and debugging
- Extensible logging architecture

### ⚙️ User Settings

- Profile and preference management
- Configurable notification and privacy options (planned)

---

## Target Audience

- Anime and manga fans
- Content creators in otaku communities
- Developers and contributors interested in community platforms

## Technology & Architecture

- **Backend** : Modular, domain-oriented design
- **Frontend** : Modern SPA with clear navigation and UX
- **Integration** : External anime/manga APIs
- **Architecture Goals** :
- Clear separation of concerns
- Maintainable DTO and entity mapping
- Support for future feature expansion
