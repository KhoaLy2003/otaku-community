# Help Center – Otaku Community Project

## Welcome

Welcome to the **Otaku Community Help Center** . This page provides guidance on how to use the platform, understand its core features, and get support when you need it.

Our goal is to help you get the most out of Otaku Community in a clear, simple, and practical way.

---

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

- Sign up using supported authentication methods
- Complete your profile to personalize your experience
- Adjust basic settings such as display name and preferences

### Navigating the Platform

- Use the main navigation menu to access Posts, Chats, and your Profile
- Unavailable or upcoming features may be marked as **Coming Soon**

---

## Posts & Content

### Creating a Post

- Write posts related to anime, manga, or otaku culture
- Add meaningful titles and clear descriptions
- Optionally link your post to related anime or manga

### Linked Anime & Manga

- Posts can reference anime or manga entities
- Linked data enriches posts with structured information
- The system may suggest links based on post content

### Managing Your Posts

- Edit your post to improve clarity or update information
- Soft delete is used to preserve discussion context

---

## Anime & Manga Data

### Data Sources

- Anime and manga information is integrated from external providers
- Metadata such as titles, images, and descriptions are synced

### Accuracy & Updates

- Data accuracy depends on external sources
- If you notice incorrect information, please report it

---

## Chat & Messaging

### Private Chats (MVP)

- One-to-one messaging is supported
- Messages are loaded using cursor-based pagination
- Deleted messages are hidden but retained internally for consistency

### Chat Safety

- Respectful communication is expected
- Abuse or spam may result in moderation actions

---

## Notifications & Activity

### Activity Tracking

- Certain actions are logged to improve system reliability
- Activity data helps diagnose issues and improve features

### Notifications (Planned)

- In-app notifications will be expanded in future releases

---

## Account & Privacy

### Account Settings

- Update profile information

### Privacy Principles

## Reporting & Moderation

### Reporting Content

- Use reporting tools to flag inappropriate or misleading content
- Reports are reviewed according to community guidelines

### Moderation Approach

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

- Page shows **404 or Coming Soon** : feature may not be available yet
- Content not loading: try refreshing or checking your connection

### Known Limitations (MVP)

- Limited chat features
- No group chats
- Some settings are not configurable yet
